vichyssoise
ingredients:
  • 8 large sweet onions, sliced thin
  • 1/2 stick (1/4 c) butter
  • 3 tbs all-purpose flour
  • 2 1/2 c quarts beef broth
  • 1/2 c vermouth
  • 5 oz cognac
  • salt & pepper to taste
  • 12 toast rounds
  • 3/4 c gruyere
instructions:
  • in a large frying pan, slowly cook the onions in the butter over low heat.
  • stir onions frequently for roughly 1 hour or until they are golden brown.
  • once brown, sprinkle the onions with flour and continue to cook for 3 minutes.
  • in a seperate pot, simmer the beef broth.
  • slowly add the broth, vermouth and cognac to the onions. season to taste.
  • stir until the soup comes to a boil. cover and let simmer for 40 minutes.
  • toast bread and top with gruyere.
  • serve onion soup in bowls with gruyere toast on top and enjoy!
