ingredients:
  • 1 1/2 c sugar
  • 1 1/2 c butter (room temperature) plus 2 tbs pan and finishing/
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 tsp. baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 1/2 c flour
  • 3 very ripe bananas
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • honey (for drizzling)
  • salt for sprinkling
instructions:
    Preheat oven to 375.
  • In a large bowl, add sugar, eggs, bananas, butter and vanilla leaving the butter still slightly chunky.
  • Sift flour, baking soda and baking powder together into the bowl and mix with wet ingredients.
  • Butter the edges of bread pan and add mixture.
  • Bake for 1 hour or until top is golden brown.
  • Drizzle honey and butter on top with a pinch of salt if desired.
